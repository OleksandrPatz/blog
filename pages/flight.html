The Alesund city

Ålesund is a remarkable destination in Norway. The town is set on a series of islands creating beautiful vistas and opportunities for outdoor activities like hiking and kayaking.

After a devastating fire in 1904, Ålesund was rebuilt in the Art Nouveau style, making its streetscapes a visual delight1.

Hiking up to viewpoints like Mount Aksla provides panoramic views of the Ålesund archipelago.

Around the Fjords

A journey where the world’s most enchanting landscapes unfold before your eyes. A trip around the Norwegian fjords is not just a holiday. it’s an expedition into the heart of nature’s masterpiece.

Sail through waters that mirror the skies, flanked by cliffs that have stood the test of time.

Away from the hustle of everyday life, the fjords provide a tranquil escape where the only agenda is to relax and rejuvenate.
